Mizzou Cloud DevOps

Training the Future Generation of Cyber Defenders

A Cyber Defense Learning Platform

Our research inspired learning emphasizes on the real-time worldwide ongoing cyber attack map that teaches the prevalent cyberattacks and defense techniques. Students can work on hands-on lab exercises to explore, execute, and evaluate cyber defense mechanisms.

Flexible Education Catalog

Mizzou Cloud DevOps features an educational catalogue of immersive, hands-on laboratory exercises on major cyber threats organized under varying difficulty levels.

Cloud-hosted and Easy to Use

It aims for easy-to-deploy cyber defense practice scenarios on a cloud by implementing a user-friendly interface with the capability to launch infrastructures with all required tools and libraries installed and ready to launch, detect and defend against an attack.

Features

Real Application Scenarios

Mizzou Cyber Range provides cyber infrastructure to students based on real application scenarios such as gaming application, healthcare application, Bio-informatics application.

Real World Cyber Attacks

Mizzou Cyber Range focuses on very on-trend attacks like Distributed Denial of Service attacks, Advanced Persistent threat attacks, Advanced Persistent Miner attack or Crypto Jacking attacks.

Research-Inspired Learning

Mizzou Cyber Range brings research and learning together to build an effective three phase pedagogical approach (Learn, Apply and Create) via cyber security learning tools. Our research-inspired learning emphasizes realistic cyber attacks and their defense techniques.
